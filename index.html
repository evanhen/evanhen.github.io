Hi, I'm Evan

Data Analyst

I am a recent graduate of Tulane University with a Bachelor of Science in Public Health and Computer Science. I currently work as a Data Analyst at Wells Fargo on the Corporate & Investment Banking TPD/CMA (Traded Products Domain/Collateral Margin and Agreements) team. My role consists of creating business requirements based on analysis and logic development, developing use cases by discerning upstream and downstream impacts, and transforming raw data into usable concepts for cross-enterprise and client needs, as well as regulatory reporting.

My work experience comprises diverse data analytics experiences, ranging from digital humanities, healthcare, and, most recently, the banking industry. Each has taught me the importance of cross-departmental collaboration, digging deeper into patterns and insights to make valuable meaning of data, and how to effectively communicate the processes and findings of data analysis to stakeholders of varying technical expertise.

My passion for data lies in promoting equity and ethical practices in the data science field, particularly at the intersection of technology and society.
